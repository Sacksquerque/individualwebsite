How It All Started

I have been doing graphic design for over 5 years now. I started in Graphic Design I class in high school and is also the class where I came up with my username, and met one of my lifelong friends. I do a wide variety of designing, from logos to doing transistions and posters. My logo, pictured, is an "S", a reference to my username's first letter and the first letter of my name, is combined with a 6, which holds deep symbolism to me, in the form of a shield, as I think of myself to do good in this world.

What I Do

I do a wide variety of designing, from gameday posters to twitter banners, to Rocket League decals and logos. I am fully self taught and I am very proud of the work that I do on a daily basis. I think my story shows that if you put your mind anything, the places you will go are unbelivable.
